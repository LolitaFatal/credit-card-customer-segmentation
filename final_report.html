4. Business Conclusions & Recommendations

Cluster 0: "Sleeping Customers" (Low Activity)

  • Observation: Lowest Balance (~1000) and lowest Purchases (~270). High volume of users.
  • Strategy: "Wake Up" Campaign.
  • Action: "Make 3 purchases this month and get 5% Cashback" OR "Log in to the App to get 500 bonus points".

Cluster 1: "The VIPs" (Big Spenders)

  • Observation: Highest Purchases (~7600) and Highest Credit Limit. They pay effectively.
  • Strategy: Retention & Loyalty. Don't lose them!
  • Action: Offer Premium Cards (Gold/Platinum), Travel benefits, or Higher Credit Limits.

Cluster 2: "Cash Users" (High Risk)

  • Observation: Massive Cash Advance usage (~4500) with low Purchases. High Balance (Debt).
  • Risk: High probability of default (bankruptcy). They use the card as an emergency ATM.
  • Action: Mitigation. Stop increasing their credit limit. Offer a structured "Personal Loan" to consolidate debt at a lower rate.

Cluster 3: "Active Spenders" (Middle Class)

  • Observation: Good Purchase activity (~1200) with low Debt (~800). They use the card wisely.
  • Strategy: Growth (Up-selling).
  • Action: Encourage larger purchases (Electronics, Installments) or Installment Plans.
